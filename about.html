About Us

Leno's Rico Taco is a Mexican resturant located in the city of Colton, California. It is a small family buisness which has been open since 1991. Leno Moreno is the owner and operator of the resturant, he is a Colton native and graduate of nearby Colton High School. Some of the most popular dishes served by the rewsturant include it's chile verde and their famous asada fries. Recently a new Drive-Thru opened allowing for quicker and easier service.
